The Raspbery Pi 5 is __not recommended__! While it is much faster, it is _exceedingly_ sensitive to power glitches and often spontaneously reboots.

You can buy the MasterPi robot with a Pi 4B and 8GB ([$380](https://www.amazon.com/LewanSoul-Robotic-Raspberry-Mecanum-MatserPi/dp/B0CLNMLK14)) 
in which case you do not need a separate Pi 4 board, heatsinks, fan, or an SD card.

The replacement batteries are optional, but increase the runtime of the robot by nearly a factor of 3. You might also want to pick up a [fast charger](https://www.amazon.com/XTAR-PB2S-Battery-Charger-Included/dp/B07WMQCWBF).

__Not listed__: 1/2" aluminum [angle stock](https://www.homedepot.com/p/Everbilt-1-2-in-x-3-ft-1-16-in-Thick-Aluminum-Angle-6442/332733650) for TOF bracket ($6).
